Professor Dr. Thomas Petschner is a renowned holistic health scientist in the field of medical facial diagnostics, correlated to the metabolic balance and genetically inherited structure of the organs. He is an internationally acknowledged researcher of complementary diagnostics and the implementation of quantum physics into medicine. 

After obtaining a Ph.D. in nutritional medicine and specialising in macrobiotics at the Kushi Institute in Boston, M.A. (1993), he spent a decade researching the diagnostic and healing methods of ancient cultures and human behaviour related to sensory responses. 

Inspired by his teacher and mentor Michio Kushi, in his studies he focused on the influence of food on embryonic development and prenatal diagnostics. In addition he developed a fascination for the field of ancient diagnostics.

Professor Dr. Petschner is director of the New Zealand based Institute for Integrated Diagnostics, affiliated to Steinbeis + Academy, an enterprise of Steinbeis University Berlin, Germany - where he provides academically recognised education.

He is also founder and CEO of Face Discovery Ltd. a company which is developing a unique facial recognition systems for the detection and prevention of illness, suicide, violence and crime.

Professor Dr. Petschner is working internationally as an independent consultant for heath care facilities, the medical device industry, corporate and government organisations. He also acts as a personal adviser for high profile individuals, actors, models, politicians and corporate leaders.

Professor Petschner is also very passionate about the implementation of humour and compassion in medicine and healthcare. His vision is to see smiles and laughter become an indispensable part of the hospital environment, given the same importance as hand washing for hygiene. 

He is the founder and CEO of Clown Doctors Asia-Pacific, Clown Doctors New Zealand (est. 2009) and Clown Doctors Singapore (est. 2014) while developing new programmes in China, Philippines and South-East Asia. 

Based on his broad experience of work in disaster zones, he established Clown Doctors Worldwide - Relief & Recovery Team, with a focus to provide physiological and mental aid for children and people in crisis situations. 

To ensure the highest standards for professional clowning are applied, he created the International Institute for Medical Clowning in 2010, the first fully specialised academic education of its kind in the world.
